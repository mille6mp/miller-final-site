Welcome to ThinkRecipe!


ThinkRecipe was created by a busy college student who was looking for an easy way to make healthy meals that wouldn't take a lot of time or money. The recipes are all designed to be made ahead of time and can be easily stored in the freezer or fridge for when you need them. There are recipes for breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks so you can always have something healthy and delicious on hand. All of the recipes are tested and approved by the ThinkRecipe team so you know they'll be good!

We know that college students are always on the go and don't always have time to cook, so we created this site to help make your life a little easier. We hope you enjoy our recipes and come back often to try new ones!

About ThinkRecipe


ThinkRecipe is a website created by college students, for college students. We know how hard it can be to find the time and energy to cook healthy meals when you're juggling classes, homework, and a social life. We believe that everybody deserves access to nutritious food, so we offer our recipes for free. We also have an educational blog where we share tips and tricks on everything from meal prepping to cooking on a budget.

Whether you're a beginner cook or a seasoned pro, we hope that you'll find something useful on our website!
